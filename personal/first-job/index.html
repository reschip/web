From Student to Professional: A Guide to Getting Your First Tech Job (Maybe…)

The path from student to professional can be intimidating. Here I share my experience and some tips for landing your first tech job.

Prepare Your Resume

A good resume is your calling card. Make sure to highlight your personal projects, even if they are small.

Networking

Never underestimate the power of connections. Participate in events, conferences, and tech community groups.

Keep Learning

Technology is constantly evolving. Stay updated and continue building projects that demonstrate your skills.
